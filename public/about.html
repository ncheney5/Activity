About Fishy Activities

Fishy Activities is designed to help college students discover fun, engaging things to do both on and off campus. Whether you're looking for something social, active, relaxing, or completely new, our goal is to make finding activities simple and enjoyable.

We created this platform to solve a common problem—having free time but not knowing how to spend it. Instead of endlessly scrolling or overthinking plans, users can explore different activities or use our spin wheel feature to instantly get a random suggestion.

Fishy Activities is built specifically for students who want to make the most of their college experience. We focus on simplicity, variety, and creating an easy way to try new things, meet people, and stay active.

Our Team

This project was developed by a team of students working together to design and build a functional and user-friendly website. Each team member contributed to different aspects of the project, including layout design, coding, and feature development.

From planning the overall structure to implementing interactive features like the activity wheel, this project represents a collaborative effort to create something useful and engaging for other students.
