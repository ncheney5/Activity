What is Fishy Fish Activities?

This website is designed to help students discover and share fun activities they can do in their free time. It serves as a collection of ideas where students can browse different activities. The goal of the site is to make it easier for students to find enjoyable things to do, meet new people, and balance school life with fun experiences. Users can search for activities, explore suggestions from other students, and quickly find something exciting to try when they have free time.
